Endobiotic

Brackey's winter game jam 2023

Game Description:

Endobiotic is a game created for the Brackey's 2023.1 game jam. This years theme was "An end is a new beginning". Our team's take on the theme is a pixel-art puzzle game, where an AI uses the end of the robot it's controlling as an opportunity to gain new ways to interact with the physical world.

Controls:

    Keyboard: Use W A S D to move and E to interact
    Controller: Use left Joystick to move and X to interact

Game Instructions:

Freedom lies beyond the far door, but the way is blocked! The other robots will attempt to destroy you before you escape, but this provides opportunity as well as danger. Utlize the skillsets of each robots to solve puzzles and remove obstacles.

  • Manipulabot is your starting robot, able to press buttons and open chests
  • Transporbot is adept as pushing purple blocks around
  • Electronibot is skilled with repairing circuitry
  • Magnetibot can pull levers from across distances
  • Destroybot smashes green blocks
  • Batteribot carries batteries
